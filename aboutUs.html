about us

The leader in the surveillance industry of Rajasthan, JK ENTERPRISES has been the largest distribution house in Rajasthan from the year 2021. Driven by its motto of "Valuing relationship, furthering growth", JK ENTERPRISES has focused on building relationships with different channel partners, system integrators, and dealers to widen the scope of the surveillance industry and increase the reach of surveillance products to each territory and household. Partnering with leading national and international brands, we have been trying to bring the reach of our partners to multiple brands and making them able to service the varied requirement of their customers. A humble beginning from an office space of 200 sq. ft. in june 2021 had never diminished our targets and our aim had always been to become a well-renowned name in the industry. Our passion along with the self-driven motivation to grow led us to become the largest distribution house of Rajasthan in 2 years' time. From a team consisting of 1 members, currently, JK ENTERPRISES has a team comprising of more than 10 employees in various domains like marketing and sales, technical support, office management, and accounts. We have made it easier for our partners to reach us though our field sales team and our digital platforms. JK ENTERPRISES , a name associated with CCTV camera, has widened its product portfolio into allied products like EPABX, Access Control, Intrusion Alarms, Video Door Phones, Fire Panels, ALL ELECTRONIC AND a complete range of accessories, etc. JK ENTERPRISES is associated as a distribution partner of more than 20 renowned national and international brands.
